Know Your Akhan Name.

In Ghana, children are given their first name (Akhan name) according to the day of the week they were born.

This application takes a birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born & depending on their gender outputs Akhan Name.

Here are the Akhan names according to days of the week and gender;

Male Day Female
kwasi Sunday Akosua
kwandwo Monday Adwoa
Kwabena Tuesday Abenna
kwaku Wednesday Aku
yaw Thursday Yaa
Kofi Friday Afua
kwame Saturday Ama
